In this section of my notes, I will describe how the internet works with HTML to transmit webpages from user to user. The content of the web are over 30 billion documents that are written in "HTML" or Hypertext Markup Language. Text is what you see and markup is how it looks. This language allows you to reference other web, images and video to provide a interactive aspect of your web page.
